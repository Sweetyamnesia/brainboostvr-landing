About Me & BrainBoostVR

Hi, I’m Angela, a solo developer and lifelong learner passionate about Virtual Reality, serious games, and cognitive training. My background combines psychology, educational digital devices, and ongoing studies in computer science, along with professional experience in multimedia, digital training, and public services.

BrainBoostVR is born from this unique mix of skills and interests. It’s a VR project designed to help users improve their cognitive abilities through immersive and interactive exercises. In the VR environment, users can walk and teleport around a virtual house, follow guided instructions, and complete exercises that test memory, attention, and problem-solving.

The project reflects my motivation to make technology engaging and accessible, and to explore innovative ways to support learning and rehabilitation. Feedback is provided in real time through colors and sounds, and users receive a final summary of their performance, including score, mistakes, and time spent.

BrainBoostVR combines my knowledge in pedagogy, UX design, and VR development, allowing me to create a safe, interactive environment that is both fun and effective for cognitive training.
