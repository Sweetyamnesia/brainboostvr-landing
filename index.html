BrainBoostVR – Rethinking Cognitive Training Through Immersive VR

A solo VR project designed to make cognitive rehabilitation more interactive, motivating, and human.

Coming Soon

Key Features

VR Navigation & Comfort

Move freely through a safe, intuitive VR environment. Users can teleport or walk, explore, and interact naturally — all while minimizing motion sickness.

Interactive Cognitive Exercises

Train memory, focus, and spatial reasoning through playful object manipulation and real-time feedback — turning rehabilitation into a motivating experience.

Real-Time Feedback & Scoring

Track progress instantly. The system records each session, offering scores and feedback to measure improvement and maintain motivation.

About BrainBoostVR

“I’ve always believed that technology should serve people — not the other way around.”

BrainBoostVR was born from this belief. Before becoming a developer, I studied psychology and earned a master’s degree in educational digital devices, where I explored how immersive technologies could enhance learning and rehabilitation. This project merges my passion for cognitive science, pedagogy, and interactive design.

I developed BrainBoostVR entirely on my own — from concept and Unity scripting to API design and testing. It represents a bridge between technology and empathy: making cognitive training more engaging, accessible, and human.

Timeline
